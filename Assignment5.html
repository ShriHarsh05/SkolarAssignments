My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Captain America: The First Avenger
    • Year Released: 2011
    • Main Actors: Chris Evans, Hayley Atwell, Sebastian Stan
    • Description: Steve Rogers, a rejected military soldier, transforms into Captain America after taking a dose of a "Super-Soldier serum". But being Captain America comes at a price as he attempts to take down a war monger and a terrorist organization.
  2. Iron Man
    • Year Released: 2008
    • Main Actors:Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Terrence Howard
    • Description: After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.
  3. Avengers
    • Year Released: 2012
    • Main Actors: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson
    • Description: Earth's mightiest heroes must come together and learn to fight as a team if they are going to stop the mischievous Loki and his alien army from enslaving humanity.
  4. Avengers: Infinity War
    • Year Released: 2018
    • Main Actors: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Mark Ruffalo
    • Description: The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.
  5. Men in Black 3 (MIB 3)
    • Year Released: 2012
    • Main Actors: Will Smith, Tommy Lee Jones, Josh Brolin
    • Description: Agent J travels in time to M.I.B.'s early years in the 1960s to stop an alien from assassinating his friend Agent K and changing history.
